About Me

Farhad Shokoohi

is a postdoctoral fellow at Department of Mathematics and Statistics at McGill University. His main research interests are Epigenetics, High Dimensional Problems, Mixture Models, Survival Analysis, Metagenomics, Bayesian Analysis, Computational Statistics, Small Area Estimation, Nonparametric Statistics and Data Mining. He was a postdoctoral fellow at McGill University and University of Manitoba, visiting scholar at The Ohio State University, and received his Ph.D. in Statistics from Shahid Beheshti University.
His hobbies include science, technology, data analysis, traditonal persian music, persian poems, computing, etc.
